# OnlyVoices

**OnlyVoices** helps online content creators **monetize their voice**. Fans and listeners can connect through **real-time voice calls**—to ask questions, have a natural conversation, or experience longer-form content like **audiobooks read in the creator’s own voice**.

The product is built around a simple idea: your voice is an asset. Creators set how listeners pay (for example, per minute of call time), clone and manage their voice, and share a public profile where people can start a call when they’re ready.

## What it enables

- **Voice monetization** — Creators earn from time spent on voice interactions instead of giving that time away for free.
- **Interactive calls** — Callers can talk with a creator’s AI-powered voice agent: questions, chat, and back-and-forth dialogue.
- **Long-form listening** — The same cloned voice can be used for narrative experiences such as audiobooks or read-aloud content, so fans hear the creator’s timbre and style.

## Who it’s for

- **Creators** who already have an audience and want a new revenue stream tied to their voice and persona.
- **Fans and users** who want a more personal, voice-first way to engage—or to consume stories and answers in a voice they already trust.
